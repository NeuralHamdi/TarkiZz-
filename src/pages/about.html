About tarkizZ

tarkizZ is a web app designed to help students stay focused while studying online. With features like task management and focus mode, tarkizZ makes it easier to stay on track and achieve your goals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help students overcome distractions and achieve their academic goals through innovative tools and features. We believe in the power of focused learning and are committed to making it accessible to everyone.
